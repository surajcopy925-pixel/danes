# Design System: Danes Clinical Wellness
**Project ID:** 9498028610902455367

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**The Modern Apothecary.** The aesthetic is one of "Clinical Warmth"—a precise, medical-grade authority balanced by high-end editorial elegance. It is unhurried, utilizing expansive negative space and a rigid grid to convey confidence and reliability. The density is intentionally low to ensure clarity and breathability.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Evergreen (#105232):** The primary brand color. Used for high-contrast headers, immersive backgrounds, and authoritative accents.
* **Pale Moss (#D8E0D1):** Secondary surface color. Used for subtle tonal shifts, internal containers, and secondary backgrounds.
* **Cream (#F8F3DF):** The main canvas. Used as the primary background for most sections to create a warm, organic feel.
* **Warm Charcoal (#2C2C2C):** Primary text color for body copy to ensure high readability without the harshness of pure black.
* **Consultation Amber (#C4922A):** Minimalist accent color. Used for status indicators, micro-CTAs, and "Gold Standard" highlights.
* **Mid Green (#19653F):** Interactive state color. Used for hover effects and active elements.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Headings (Copperplate Gothic):** Always Uppercase with generous tracking (+10% to +15%). This font provides the "Clinical" anchor—authoritative and timeless.
* **Body (Jost Light 300):** Primary font for long-form reading. The Light weight provides an airy, premium feel.
* **Functional Labels (Jost Regular 400):** Used for micro-copy, form labels, and functional metadata.
* **Navigation/Sub-headings (Jost Medium 500):** Provides structural weight and clarity.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Rectangular with a minimal 4px radius. Primary buttons are Evergreen with White text; secondary buttons use a "Ghost" style with a 20% opacity border. Hover transitions to Mid Green.
* **Cards/Containers:** Defined by **Background Color Blocking** rather than borders. Corner roundness is strictly limited to 4px. No drop shadows are allowed.
* **Inputs/Forms:** Minimalist underline or Pale Moss background fill. Active state uses a 1px bottom border in Consultation Amber.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Whitespace:** Extreme generous margins and padding. Sections should feel "empty" rather than cluttered.
* **Alignment:** Rigid grid alignment to maintain a "Clinical" sense of order.
* **Tonal Blocking:** Transitions between sections are achieved by shifting between Cream, Pale Moss, and Evergreen surfaces.
* **Responsiveness:** Mobile-first design optimized for 390px, scaling gracefully to 1440px desktop layouts.
